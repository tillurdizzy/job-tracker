New Job

The JOBS page is where you create, track and update the roofing jobs you currently have in progress.

To create a new job, you first need:

  1. A Client - create a new client under the CLIENTS tab, or use an existing one.
  2. A Property - create a new property for the Client under the PROPERTIES tab.
  3. After you have a Client and a Property, click the New Job (+) button above.

On the left edge of each Job listing is a red containing the Job Status . All new Jobs are created with the Status of Prospect , and progresses through successive stages as follows:

  • Proposal - Bid and Scope of Work have been created and submitted to client. Waiting approval.
  • Contract - Bid and Scope of Work have been approved and accepted by client, and initial payment has been received.
  • Active - Materials have been delivered and a crew is on the job site.
  • Complete - All payments has been received.

Click the green Pencil Icon at the end of each listing to create a Proposal, update the Status, or make other changes.
