The Jobs Summary page is your main entryway into the program, and consists of a listing of all the jobs you currently have in progress.

On the left edge, in the red box, is the Job Status . All new Jobs are created with the Status of Prospect , and progresses through successive stages as follows:

  • Proposal - Bid and Scope of Work have been created and submitted to client. Waiting approval.
  • Contract - Bid and Scope of Work have been approved and accepted by client, and initial payment has been received.
  • Active - Materials have been delivered and a crew is on the job site.
  • Complete - All payments has been received.

Click the green Pencil Icon at the end of each listing to make changes and edits to the Job.
