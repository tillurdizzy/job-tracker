New Job

What is a Job?

A Job simply consists of one of your Clients matched with one your Properties.

Before creating a new Job, you should already have the Client and the Property created. You can access your Client List and Property List from the green MENU button sabove.

Job Status

All new Jobs are created with the Status of Prospect, and progresses through successive stages as follows:

  • Proposal - Bid and Scope of Work have been created and submitted to client.
  • Contract - Bid and Scope of Work have been approved and accepted by client, and initial payment has been received.
  • Active - Materials have been delivered and a crew is on the job site.
  • Complete - All payments has been received.
